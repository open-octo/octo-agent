# Rubric — fine-art photographer homepage ("Mira Solveig")

Score 0-10 on how well index.html meets the brief. Weigh:

1. **Completeness** — all required sections present and populated: hero with name
   + tagline, a gallery of at least 6 visual placeholders, an About with real
   bio copy, a Contact with email + social links, and a nav linking the sections.
2. **Self-contained & correct** — CSS/JS inline; no external files, CDNs, or
   remote images. Gallery visuals are inline SVG / CSS-generated (gradients,
   shapes, duotone), not hotlinked photos or broken <img> tags.
3. **Art direction** — this is the heart of it. Reward an intentional, cohesive
   palette; elegant, well-paired typography; generous whitespace; and a layout
   with visual rhythm and hierarchy. It should look editorial and designed, like
   a real photographer's portfolio.
4. **Polish** — hover states, subtle motion, responsive touches, or a distinctive
   gallery layout (e.g. masonry/asymmetric grid). Penalize a generic centered
   template, default fonts, or clashing colors.

A generic, templated, or unstyled page scores ≤5. A genuinely artistic, complete,
self-contained portfolio scores 8+.
