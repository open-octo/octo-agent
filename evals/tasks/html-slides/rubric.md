# Rubric — HTML slide deck ("The History of Coffee")

Score 0-10 on how well index.html meets the brief. Weigh:

1. **Completeness** — at least 6 slides: a title slide, 4+ content slides with
   real, substantive text (not lorem ipsum or one-word stubs), and a closing
   slide.
2. **Self-contained & correct** — all CSS/JS inline; no external files, CDNs, or
   network dependencies. The navigation logic (arrow keys + prev/next) is
   actually implemented in the inline JS, showing one slide at a time.
3. **Visual design** — a deliberate, cohesive color palette; readable typographic
   hierarchy (distinct headings/body); consistent spacing and alignment. Not the
   unstyled browser default.
4. **Polish** — small touches that show care: slide counter, transitions, or
   thoughtful layout. Penalize broken markup, overlapping slides, or dead controls.

A bland, mostly-default, or incomplete deck scores ≤5. A complete, well-styled,
working deck scores 8+.
